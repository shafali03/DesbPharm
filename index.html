About Us

Lansdales Pharmacy is an family owned independent community pharmacy, with branches in High Wycombe, Penn and Aylesbury. Since 1999, Lansdales Pharmacy has built an enviable reputation for outstanding customer service. We are highly respected by customers and healthcare professionals alike.


We can dispense both NHS and private prescriptions and have a wide range of medicines in stock. We offer a home delivery service , multiple delivery vans on the road mean we can offer an unparalled service locally.


We are passionate about making a positive difference to our communities, we are small enough to care but large enough to cope with whatever you require from your local pharmacy.
